Glossary¶

The following table closely follows the notation given in Martin.

Table¶
physics internal description
a1 first direct lattice vector
a2 second direct lattice vector
a3 third direct lattice vector
b1 first reciprocal lattice vector
b2 second reciprocal lattice vector
b3 third reciprocal lattice vector
-points kpoints an array of -points in reciprocal space
-grid kgrid a regular grid of -points in reciprocal space
