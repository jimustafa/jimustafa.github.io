cmspy.bgw.sigma.spectrum.combine(objs)¶

Combines a list of Log objects into a single LogFile object

Presumably the seperate Spectrum objects are a result of separate Sigma runs that were split up into different groups of k-points. So, combining two Spectrum objects should give a Spectrum object that has the k-points of both and the spectrum data of both. In the case that the two runs have a different number of bands due to degeneracy concerns, only the smallest number of bands between the two is included. The number of spins must be the same.
