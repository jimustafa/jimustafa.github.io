About¶

OPFm.py is an open-source Python library implementing the Optimized Projection Functions Method.

Development¶

The primary development of the package was carried out by Jamal I. Mustafa with the help of Sinisa Coh at UC Berkeley. At the time, Jamal was a graduate student in the group of Steven G. Louie and Sinisa was a postdoc.
